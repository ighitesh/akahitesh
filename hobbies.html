Hobbies

Photography

Brief

Honestly, I don't know when this became my hobby. It all started when I began capturing pictures of breathtaking landscapes, captivating portraits, and mesmerizing cloud formations. I found myself drawn to the art of photography, experimenting with different subjects and exploring the intricacies of both natural and artificial lighting. Photography has become a passion that continuously fuels my desire to explore, learn, and capture the beauty that surrounds me.

 

Watching Netflix

Brief

I like watching Netflix, and when it comes to choosing between movies and series, I tend to lean towards movies. The reason for this preference is that once I start watching a series, I often find myself irresistibly drawn into it, and before I know it, I end up binge-watching the entire series to completion. However, with movies, I can enjoy a complete story in one sitting.

 

Travelling

Brief

I like to travel very much as it fills me with complete joy and happiness. Additionally, meeting new people allows me to learn many new things about life and gain different perspectives on the journey. Moreover, the beauty of the places I visit often mesmerizes me, making me wish I could stay there longer.

 

Listening Songs

Brief

Listening to music allows me to completely detach from the world. I love listening to Hindi songs a lot, but you may be surprised to know that I don't have any specific playlist for them. Initially, I attempted to create a playlist for the best songs, but soon I realized that most Hindi songs are my favorites. Therefore, I find it better to listen to every song rather than limiting myself to a playlist.
